To enable the SeaDark Theme in Visual FoxPro:
 - Place the files "SeaDarkVFP.FPX" and "SeaDarkVFP.PRG" in the default file folder;
 - Open Visual FoxPro;
 - Type the command "DO SeaDarkVFP" and press <ENTER> to close the message on [OK];
 - Close Visual FoxPro;
 - Open Visual FoxPro again;
 - Click on the "Tools" tab in the top menu;
 - Click on "Options..." inside the "Tools" tab;
 - Click on the "Editor" tab;
 - Click on the [Set As Default] button;
 - Click [OK];
 - Ready Sea Dark Theme active in Visual FoxPro!

To enable SeaDark Theme in NotePad++:
 - Locate the NotePad++ folder;
 -- Right-clicking on the NotePad++ executable and "Open file location";
 or go to the path "C:\Program Files (x86)\Notepad++";
 - Enter the "themes" folder and place the "SeaDark.XML" file;
 - Open NotePad++ ;
 - Click on the "Settings" tab in the top menu;
 - Click wm "Style configurator..." inside the "Settings" tab;
 - In the combo "Select theme:" select the theme "SeaDark";
 - Ready Sea Dark Theme active in NotePad++!
